## Executive Summary

Charlotte's skyline features the **Duke Energy Center** as its primary LED-illuminated landmark building. The building uses approximately 2,500 programmable color-changing LED lights that create dynamic light shows visible throughout Uptown Charlotte. Several other major buildings participate in coordinated lighting programs for special events and causes.

## Charlotte LED Lighting Programs

### "Light the Night" Community Initiative
- Multiple buildings coordinate colored lighting for causes
- Participating: BOA Corporate Center, Duke Energy Center, Truist Center, Wells Fargo, Hearst Tower, Carillon Tower, Ally Center

### "Wells Fargo Lights" Program
- Year-round recognition of community events and causes
- Recently expanded with $500M Charlotte investment
- New 14-foot illuminated Wells Fargo sign added 2025

### Carolina Panthers Integration
- Duke Energy Center visible from Bank of America Stadium
- Lights blue (Process Blue) for Panthers touchdowns
- Real-time integration during games

### ACC Championship Integration
- Duke Energy Center illuminates in scoring team's colors

## Technical Notes for Digital Twin Recreation

### Duke Energy Center LED Characteristics
- **Position**: Vertical strips running up facade
- **Colors**: Full RGB spectrum
- **Default**: White
- **Common Colors**: Blue (Panthers), red/green (Christmas), team colors
- **Animation**: Static colors for events, not animated patterns
- **Intensity**: Visible from several miles (visible from stadium)

### Building Proportions for Reference
| Building | Relative Height | LED Feature |
|----------|----------------|-------------|
| BOA Corporate Center | Tallest (871 ft) | Participates in programs |
| Duke Energy Center | 2nd tallest (786.5 ft) | Primary LED system |
| Truist Center | 3rd (659 ft) | Signage + programs |
| One Wells Fargo | 4th (588 ft) | Rounded top feature |
| Carillon Tower | Shorter (394 ft) | Distinctive spire |

### LED Color Reference
- **Panthers Process Blue**: #0085CA (approximate)
- **Default White**: Warm white (~3000K)
- **Christmas**: Red (#FF0000), Green (#00FF00)
- **Custom Events**: Variable RGB
