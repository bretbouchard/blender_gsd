2. `_calc_over_shoulder()` - Behind with shoulder offset
3. `_calc_chase()` - Speed-based distance adjustment
4. `_calc_chase_side()` - Side angle for racing
5. `_calc_orbit_follow()` - Continuous orbit rotation
6. `_calc_lead()` - Camera ahead of subject
7. `_calc_aerial()` - Top-down view
8. `_calc_free_roam()` - Free movement with collision

- `calculate_camera_rotation()` - Look-at calculation
- `get_target_forward_direction()` - Velocity-based forward
