- All 4 light types: area, spot, point, sun
- Area light shapes: SQUARE, RECTANGLE, DISK, ELLIPSE
- Color temperature support (Blender 4.0+)
- Light linking for selective illumination (Blender 4.0+)
- Preset inheritance with `extends` field resolution
- Angle/distance positioning with look-at rotation
