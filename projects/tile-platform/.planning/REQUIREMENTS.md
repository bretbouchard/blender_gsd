# Requirements: Mechanical Tile Platform

## v1 Requirements

### PLATFORM - Core Platform

**PLATFORM-01: Platform Extension via Tile Placement**
The platform can extend by placing tiles at specified positions.
- **Category:** PLATFORM
- **Priority:** Critical
- **Phase:** 1
- **Status:** Pending

**PLATFORM-02: Platform Retraction via Tile Removal**
The platform can retract by removing tiles and storing arms underneath.
- **Category:** PLATFORM
- **Priority:** Critical
- **Phase:** 1
- **Status:** Pending

---

### TILE - Tile System

**TILE-01: Magneto-Mechanical Tile Connection**
Tiles connect via magneto-mechanical side mounting with visual feedback.
- **Category:** TILE
- **Priority:** High
- **Phase:** 2
- **Status:** Pending

**TILE-02: Configurable Tile Shapes**
System supports multiple tile shapes (square, octagonal, hexagonal).
- **Category:** TILE
- **Priority:** Medium
- **Phase:** 2
- **Status:** Pending

---

### ARM - Mechanical Arm System

**ARM-01: Physics-Simulated Arm Movement**
Arms use physics simulation for natural movement.
- **Category:** ARM
- **Priority:** High
- **Phase:** 3
- **Status:** Pending

**ARM-02: Constrained Arm Targeting**
Arms are constrained to always reach their target positions.
- **Category:** ARM
- **Priority:** Critical
- **Phase:** 4
- **Status:** Pending

**ARM-03: Arm Folding Mechanism**
Arms fold into compact storage under the platform when idle.
- **Category:** ARM
- **Priority:** High
- **Phase:** 5
- **Status:** Pending

---
